Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way for you to connect with me?

Scheduling a 1:1 Strategic Clarity Call with me is the best way to connect. This time together allows us to connect and dedicate time to discuss your needs. For urgent matters or specific proposals, email works too at paul@paultakisaki.com.

How fast do you typically respond?

I would say I respond to all emails within 24 hours on business days. When we have a scheduled call, I use a reliable and dedicated calendar to keep my commitments and I always show up prepared. And right now, active coaching clients can expect same day priorities.

Do you offer free consultations?

The Strategic Clarity Call is free. It's a 30-minute focused call to understand your situation and to assess whether or not we're a good fit. There's no pitch, only the honest assessment and recommended actions from us.

What if I'm not really sure of what service I need?

This is not uncommon! In fact, many clients are often not exactly sure what services or actions they need, they just want a better outcome, of some sort. In our call we will diagnose your situation and recommend the best path, whether it's coaching, consulting or something else altogether. I will work with you to help identify where you are now, where you want to go and the fastest way to get there.
