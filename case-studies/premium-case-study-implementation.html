It was especially damaging during the Fixed Wireless Access (FWA) product push—a massive growth opportunity that was falling flat due to confusion around positioning, technical validation, and execution. The rules of engagement (ROE) were a full page of font size 7 that no one actually followed.

"The transformation Paul led gave our reps the one thing they were missing: clarity. By simplifying structure, aligning incentives, and removing noise, he built a system we could trust—and a path to win."

The Solution

Paul implemented a three-part solution that transformed the organization:

  1. Content Consolidation & Clarity - Reduced 87 conflicting resources into one single source of truth
  2. Enablement Function Launch - Created a team of 6 enablement pros responsible for owning resource quality
  3. POD Structure & Incentive Alignment - Designed a simplified ROE with role clarity and shared recognition

What made the rollout stick wasn't just process—it was trust. Paul involved the sales directors directly in the pod design. He pulled in team influencers early, asked for input, gave visibility, and made them part of the build.

The transformation gave reps the one thing they were missing: clarity. By simplifying structure, aligning incentives, and removing noise, Paul gave the organization a system they could trust—and a path to win.
