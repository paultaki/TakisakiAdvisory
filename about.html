The Handlebar Test

Remember riding a bike without handlebars as a kid? That first moment feels like certain doom—your heart races, your stomach drops, and every instinct screams to grab hold again. But if you resist that impulse, something magical happens: the bike continues forward with remarkable stability, and you experience a freedom unlike anything before.

Leadership requires this same counterintuitive leap. When I took over struggling territories, I didn't tighten control—I let go of the handlebars. I delegated 80% of decisions, focused on the vital few metrics, and trusted my team's strengths.

The result? Historic records broken. Top-line revenue up 37% year-over-year. Net customer additions up 96%. And for the first time, a team that came to work energized, aligned, and ready to win.
