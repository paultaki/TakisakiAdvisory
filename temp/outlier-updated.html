| Leadership Principles

1

80/20 Focus

Ruthlessly prioritizing the vital few initiatives that drive 80% of results, ensuring maximum impact with minimal wasted effort.

2

Strength-Based Teams

Designing teams around individual strengths rather than trying to fix weaknesses, placing people where their natural talents create maximum value.

3

Trust & Delegation

Empowering teams by "letting go of the handlebars" and trusting their expertise, providing guidance on the destination while allowing flexibility in the journey.

4

ROI-Driven Decisions

Ensuring every initiative has measurable return on investment and tracking results, maintaining a disciplined focus on business impact over activity.
