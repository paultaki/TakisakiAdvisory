Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does this require?

The system is designed for busy leaders, requiring approximately 10 hours per week over 90 days. The templates streamline implementation, making the time investment highly efficient.

Does this work for different team sizes?

Yes. For small teams (under 20), you can meet with everyone. For larger organizations, use the stratified approach in the system: 25% high-potential, 25% random sampling, 25% from problem areas, and 25% self-nominated. The system scales from small teams to 500+ person organizations.

What if my team has low engagement?

The strength-based approach is particularly effective for disengaged teams. By focusing on what people are naturally good at and realigning roles accordingly, you'll see motivation increase naturally. The 'Early Win' phase specifically targets engagement through visible improvements.

How is this different from other leadership programs?

Most leadership programs focus on theory or leader behaviors. The 90-Day Accelerator centers on uncovering and deploying team strengths through a structured, practical system. It's built from real-world success in multiple turnaround scenarios, not academic theory.

What if I'm inheriting a turnaround situation?

This system was built specifically for turnarounds. The diagnostic phase helps you identify which stage your team is in, and the templates adapt accordingly. The Pacific Northwest case study demonstrates how to apply this in a challenging turnaround scenario.

Do I need to wait until I'm new to a role?

No. While designed for leadership transitions, this system works equally well for established leaders looking to transform team performance. Simply adjust the timeline for the introduction phase if your team already knows you.
