Fixer File #001

The Civil War Turnaround

Reps were calling me begging to switch teams. HR was drowning. Directors wouldn't speak. I had to rebuild belief, fast.

🕵️ INTEL BRIEF:

  • ⏰ Timeline: Q1–Q4 20XX
  • 🎯 Goal: Culture Unification + Revenue Growth
  • 👥 Team: 100+ (Directors, ADs, Reps)
  • 🔥 Outcome: #1 Org Nationally | Zero HR Cases | 40% Attrition Drop

🔍 THE DIAGNOSIS:

  • Leadership silos + turf wars
  • Zero mission, zero unity, zero psychological safety
  • HR investigations + passive-aggressive sabotage
  • Reps skipping managers to talk to me directly
  • Org chart ≠ Influence map. Trust was gone.
“This wasn’t a strategy problem—it was a belief problem.”

💣 TACTICAL STRIKES:

  • 📝 Reset the Mission: Unified mantra: "Build the team everyone wants to join." Reps helped write it.
  • 🤝 Forced Collaboration: Paired rivals, created "Truth Room" sessions, praise requirements across teams.
  • ☎️ Opened Frontline Access: Took every call, 3 layers down. "Paul listens" spread like wildfire.
  • 🧠 Strategic Coaching: Rewired leaders to stop asking me. New loop: "What do your peers think?"
  • 🛏️ Cut Poison, Not People: Named and cleared out toxic behaviors. Some self-selected out.

📊 THE OUTCOME:

  • 🏆 #1 Ranked Org in the Nation
  • 💬 "You were the first leader to make them act like a team."
  • ✉️ HR Cases → Zero in 6 months
  • 🙏 Engagement +21%
  • ⬇ Voluntary Attrition ↓ 40%
  • ✨ Became a landing pad for top talent
“They still didn’t love each other... but they fought together.”

🫠 PAUL'S LESSON:

"Love doesn’t always look like hugs. Sometimes it’s structure, standards, and listening when nobody else will. Leadership isn’t about being liked. It’s about building belief."
