About Me

Quiet competitor. Culture architect. Chess player in a checkers world.

From Outsider to Operator

I didn't take the traditional route. I failed a semester, mowed lawns for cash, and landed CEO meetings before I could legally drive. While others followed the script, I questioned the whole playbook.

At Verizon, I turned broken markets into record-breaking teams. Not by applying more pressure—but by building trust and teaching strategy. I didn't just see where we needed to go—I mapped the moves, won buy-in from the front line up, and stayed in it until the results came through.

My philosophy is simple, but rarely followed: Show love. Lead with clarity. Build what lasts. When you do that, performance becomes personal. Loyalty becomes legacy.
