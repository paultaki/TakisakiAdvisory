🔍 What’s a Pull Quote? A pull quote is a short, powerful excerpt or quote from someone else (or even your own writing) that’s pulled out of the body content and styled to stand out visually. Think of it like a billboard in the middle of your story. 🧠 Why They Work Break up long content with visual interest Add credibility with real voices Highlight key moments without making people read everything Perfect for showcasing what others said about your leadership mid-case-study, sales page, or testimonial block 🔧 What They Look Like (Examples): 🔹 In a Fixer File “You helped me become the authentic leader I am now... I’ll be seeing you at the helm of a successful business.” — Joy Lunger, Director, Verizon 🔹 On a Homepage “Paul’s system changed how we led.” — Todd Mundy, Sr. Director 🔹 Inside a Slide or PDF “You’ll always be the guy who knows playing to win better than anyone else.” — Chris Shank, VP They’re often formatted in italics or set
