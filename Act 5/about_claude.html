Precision Frameworks

01

The 80/20 Leadership OS

Identify what actually moves the needle, focus ruthlessly, and ignore the noise. When you're not chasing every metric, you master the critical few.

I asked my team: "Will you get fired if you don't deliver on this metric?" For 80% of what consumed their time, the answer was no.

I communicated clearly: "If we can be the best in these 3-4 metrics, I'll cover you on the other 15; you won't even hear about them."

The result: Record-breaking performance without burnout. We focused on what actually mattered, ignored the rest, and outperformed every other team in the company.

02

The Handlebar Test

Building self-reliant teams means removing your grip. Let go of control, and watch capability flourish. The key is building the right systems first.

I delegated 80% of decisions and focused only on the vital few that actually drove results.

I stopped reviewing every email, rewriting presentations, and micromanaging deals.

The result: Teams that didn't need me to function. Leaders who could make decisions. Systems that scaled beyond my personal bandwidth.

03

The First 90 Days Protocol

From outsider to catalyst in three months. Skip the politics, find the truth, build trust at speed, and identify the one high-impact fix that signals change.

I booked 1:1s with every single employee, starting at the edge. No agenda—just their story, their pain points, their ideas.

I identified one fast, high-impact fix based on frontline feedback and got it done immediately.

The result: Trust built at speed. Problems surfaced that leadership never knew existed. Solutions that worked because they came from the people closest to the work.

04

Strategic ROI-Driven Initiatives

10x returns on targeted bets. Build the business case, track the metrics that matter, and create programs that pay for themselves many times over.

I developed a cupcake program that finance thought was ridiculous—until it delivered a 10x ROI during the pilot phase.

I turned customer success from a cost center into a revenue-protection engine that saved $53M+ in its first year.

The result: Programs that scaled because they paid for themselves, creating a virtuous cycle of investment and return.
