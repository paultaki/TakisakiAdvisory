Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I'm ready for executive coaching?

You're ready for coaching if you're facing leadership challenges, preparing for a promotion, or seeking to elevate your impact. The best candidates are open to feedback, committed to growth, and willing to implement new approaches. If you feel like you've reached a plateau or are ready to accelerate your development, coaching is likely a good fit.

What's the difference between coaching and consulting?

Consulting provides specific solutions to business problems, while coaching focuses on developing your leadership capabilities and decision-making. My approach blends both—providing frameworks and strategies while developing your ability to navigate complex situations independently. I don't just tell you what to do; I help you develop the skills to solve problems for yourself.

How long does it take to see results?

Many clients report immediate benefits in clarity and confidence after our first few sessions. Tangible business results typically emerge within 1-3 months as you implement new approaches. Leadership transformation is a journey—we'll track progress with clear metrics aligned to your goals. The most sustainable results come from the 3-month program, which allows time for new habits and approaches to take root.

Do you only work with executives?

I work with established executives, rising leaders, and high-potential managers ready to accelerate their growth. The common thread is ambition, openness to growth, and a desire to lead with purpose and impact. My clients range from directors preparing for VP roles to executives looking to expand their influence and legacy.
