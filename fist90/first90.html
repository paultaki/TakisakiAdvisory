The 4 Phases

Phase 1: Human Introduction (Weeks 1-2)

Objective: Build trust through vulnerability, strengths.

Task: Authentic Introduction

Why: Teams follow stories.

  • Share struggle (e.g., mall kiosk) with photos.
  • Admit limits: "I'm here for your strengths."
  • Express: "I joined to build on your talents."

Script: "I'm Paul, a father who started door-to-door, doubting I'd make it. Leadership is unlocking your strengths. What's your story?"

Phase 2: Intelligence Gathering (Weeks 3-4)

Objective: Diagnose, map strengths, prioritize metrics.

Tasks:

  • Diagnose Territory: Turnaround, Growth, Stability, Startup.
  • Meet 10-20 Customers: "What's working? What would you change?"
  • Engage Influencers: Identified from 1:1s.
  • Triangulate Truth: Align priorities.

Phase 3: Early Win (Weeks 5-8)

Objective: Deliver strength-based wins.

Tasks:

  • Secure 2-3 Visible Wins: Solvable, impactful.
  • Hero Request: Solve "no."
  • Calibration Calls: 30-min highlights.

Phase 4: Strategic Direction (Weeks 9-12)

Objective: Inspire vision.

Tasks:

  • Develop Vision: Five Questions framework.
  • Vital Few Scorecard: "3 metrics."
  • 7 Communication Channels: All-hands, 1:1s, etc.
  • Strength-Based Implementation: Ownership.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does this require?

The system requires approximately 10 hours per week over 90 days. The templates streamline implementation, making the time investment highly efficient.

Does this work for different team sizes?

Yes. For small teams (under 20), you can meet with everyone. For larger organizations, use the stratified approach: 25% high-potential, 25% random sampling, 25% from problem areas, and 25% self-nominated.

What if my team has low engagement?

The strength-based approach is particularly effective for disengaged teams. By focusing on what people are naturally good at and realigning roles accordingly, you'll see motivation increase naturally.

What if I'm inheriting a turnaround situation?

This system was built specifically for turnarounds. The diagnostic phase helps you identify which stage your team is in, and the templates adapt accordingly.

Do I need to wait until I'm new to a role?

No. While designed for leadership transitions, this system works equally well for established leaders looking to transform team performance.
